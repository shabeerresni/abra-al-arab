Our Services

Abra Al Arab provides comprehensive energy and commodities trading services, connecting reliable producers with customers across the Middle East, Africa, and Asia. We specialize in the trading and distribution of refined oil products, crude oil, diesel, lubricants, tar, and asphalt through safe, efficient, and compliant supply chains.

Refined Oil Products

We trade and distribute a comprehensive range of refined petroleum products to meet diverse market needs.

  • Gasoline
  • Kerosene
  • Jet Fuel
  • Naphtha
  • Gas Oil

Crude Oil

We facilitate the trading of crude oil from various producing regions to refineries and end-users globally.

  • Light Crude
  • Heavy Crude
  • Sweet Crude
  • Sour Crude

Diesel

We supply high-quality diesel fuel for industrial, commercial, and transportation applications.

  • Ultra-Low Sulfur Diesel
  • Automotive Diesel
  • Marine Diesel
  • Industrial Diesel

Lubricants

We distribute premium lubricants and base oils for automotive, industrial, and marine applications.

  • Engine Oils
  • Industrial Lubricants
  • Marine Lubricants
  • Base Oils
  • Greases

Tar & Asphalt

We supply bitumen, tar, and asphalt products for construction, road building, and industrial applications.

  • Bitumen
  • Road Asphalt
  • Industrial Tar
  • Modified Bitumen

Supply Chain Solutions

We provide end-to-end supply chain management services to ensure efficient and reliable delivery.

  • Logistics Coordination
  • Storage Solutions
  • Quality Assurance
  • Documentation & Compliance
  • Risk Management

Supporting Capabilities
