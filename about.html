About Rayenna International

Who We Are

Rayenna International is a leading energy and commodities trading company based in Dubai, United Arab Emirates. We specialize in connecting reliable producers of refined oil products, crude oil, diesel, lubricants, tar, and asphalt with customers across the Middle East, Africa, and Asia.

Established with a commitment to reliability, ethics, and dependability, we have built a reputation as a trusted partner in the global energy trading marketplace. Our extensive network and deep industry expertise enable us to provide comprehensive trading solutions that meet the diverse needs of our clients.

We operate with the highest standards of compliance, ensuring that all our transactions adhere to international regulations and best practices. Our rigorous internal processes include comprehensive KYC procedures, sanctions screening, credit assessment, and HSE standards, consistent with global commodity trading practices.

What We Do

At Rayenna International, we facilitate the safe, efficient, and compliant movement of petroleum products across international markets. Our core activities include:

  • → Trading & Distribution: We source and distribute a wide range of petroleum products including refined oil, crude oil, diesel, lubricants, tar, and asphalt.
  • → Supply Chain Management: We design and manage efficient supply chains that ensure timely delivery while maintaining cost-effectiveness.
  • → Market Intelligence: We provide our clients with valuable insights into market trends, pricing, and supply-demand dynamics.
  • → Compliance & Risk Management: We ensure all transactions comply with international regulations and implement robust risk management practices.
  • → Relationship Management: We build and maintain strong relationships with producers, suppliers, and customers across our operating regions.

Our operations span key markets in the Middle East, Africa, and Asia, allowing us to serve a diverse clientele including refiners, distributors, industrial consumers, and government entities.
